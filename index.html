Basics of VGC 2020

May 21, 2020 by Watrr

This upcoming report will be a short guide to VGC 2020.

VGC 2020 is the very first format with Dynamax. Dynamax doubles the HP stat of the one pokemon that you dynamax for 3 turns. Both players are allowed to dynamax once per battle. Some pokemon tend to dynamax more than others. Most teams only have 1-2 dynamax threats on their team. Being able to identify those threats and shut them down is optimal. To position yourself to win games you have to dynamax the most optimal pokemon at the most optimal time. One major way to ensure your dynamax lasts the full 3 turns is my have a user that can redirect attacks using follow me. Teams consist of the 6 pokemon from the Galar dex #1-397.
